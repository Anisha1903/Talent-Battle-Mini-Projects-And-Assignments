Hostel

The Hostel Complex has been built within the campus. Two independent blocks are provided for boys and girl students. All the hostels encompass beautiful gardens. Each hostel has resident rector to monitor and to maintain the discipline. Hostel rooms are airy, clean and well furnished. Amrutvahini College of Engineering, ranked among Top 100 college of India as per survey of CSR and other national agency, and is situated on the NH-50 with mountain views, clean, safe surrounding area and vast open spaces all around its 50-acre campus. Its top-notch campus has well-planned green spaces and lanes. The residential complex is hailed as one world-class hostel facilities with spacious and large rooms, an RO system is installed for students and housekeeping cleans the rooms every day. Around 1700 students live here. A fully-residential campus, the hostels are named after popular Forts of Maharashtra. There are two boy’s hostels and two are the girls’ hostel too. Four students can accommodate in one room, hostel management provide one bed, table, chair and cub board to every student. Each hostel has its own dining mess, where breakfast, lunch and dinner are served for students. Each AVCOE hostel has its own study room, TV room, Guest Room, garden, gymkhana, commercial complex and an on-campus clinic with highly experienced doctors for boys and girls hostel separately. AVCOE have its own Ambulance for any medical emergency.
